# Governance

Cella is maintained by **Awen LLC** on behalf of the **Cardano Curia** Constitutional Committee consortium. This document describes how decisions get made.

## Roles

- **Maintainers** , review and merge contributions, triage issues and security reports, cut releases, and steward the roadmap. Current maintainers are listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).
- **Contributors** , anyone who opens issues or pull requests.

## Decision making

- **Day-to-day changes:** lazy consensus among maintainers via pull-request review. A PR may merge once a maintainer approves and CI passes.
- **Significant or breaking changes:** discussed in an issue first; maintainers seek consensus before implementation.
- **Disagreements:** resolved by the lead maintainer (see [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md)), in the interest of the project and the consortium it serves.

## Roadmap & priorities

The roadmap lives in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md) and is aligned with milestones under the Intersect Open Source Office Tooling Sustainability Program. Scope stays focused on sustaining the Constitutional Committee workflow , maintainability, developer/operator experience, interoperability, and security , not net-new product directions.

## Becoming a maintainer

Sustained, high-quality contributions can lead to a maintainer invitation from the existing maintainers.

## Changing this document

Governance changes are proposed via pull request and require maintainer approval.
